Learning Design Solution
Short Video Modules

To make the learning process engaging and accessible, we developed cooking tutorials broken down into bite-sized video modules. Each recipe is divided into manageable steps, presented in short videos to maintain attention and support easy follow-along. Modules are categorized by skill level (beginner, intermediate, advanced) and include estimated cooking times (e.g., “30 minutes”) to help students choose meals that match their time and ability.
Personalized Experience

To encourage students to begin cooking and stay motivated, the platform offers a personalized learning journey. Through an initial onboarding survey, users receive tailored recipe recommendations based on their preferences, dietary needs, and skill level. An integrated AI chatbot supports users throughout the experience—offering real-time guidance and instant feedback on their final dishes.
Professional Feedback and Community

To foster a sense of connection and growth, the platform features opportunities for both expert and peer engagement. Students can receive one-on-one feedback from professional chefs via direct messages and share their completed dishes within the community space. This allows for peer comparison, encouragement, and feedback—making learning collaborative and socially rewarding.
